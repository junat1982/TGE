About Us

Built on Expertise.
Driven by Partnership.

Transglobe Energy is a New York-based specialty chemical distribution and global trade company with a singular focus: making complex international supply chains perform with precision. We act as a strategic bridge between world-class chemical producers and the industrial buyers who depend on consistent, high-quality inputs to operate.

Our team combines deep technical knowledge of chemical markets with hands-on expertise in international logistics, trade compliance, and commercial negotiation. This dual competency allows us to offer more than sourcing — we provide end-to-end supply chain intelligence that reduces risk, shortens lead times, and creates measurable cost efficiencies for our clients.

We serve manufacturers, formulators, and trading houses across North America, Asia Pacific, Europe, and the Middle East — operating with the flexibility of a specialist firm and the reach of a global network. Whether you require spot procurement or long-term supply agreements, Transglobe Energy delivers with reliability and commercial integrity.

Our Products

A Portfolio Built
for Industry

We maintain an extensive and continuously evolving portfolio spanning twelve chemical categories. Each product is sourced from vetted, globally certified manufacturers — ensuring consistent specification, regulatory compliance, and uninterrupted availability for our customers' production requirements.

01 🧪
Benzyl Products

Aromatic compounds widely used in fragrances, pharmaceuticals, coatings, and chemical synthesis. Sourced to high-purity grades for exacting industrial applications.

  • Benzyl Alcohol
  • Benzaldehyde
  • Benzyl Chloride
  • Benzyl Acetate
  • Benzyl Benzoate
02 💧
Glycols

Essential intermediates for polyester resins, antifreeze, HVAC systems, and personal care formulations. Available across the full MEG–TEG and propylene glycol spectrum.

  • Monoethylene Glycol (MEG)
  • Diethylene Glycol (DEG)
  • Triethylene Glycol (TEG)
  • Propylene Glycol (PGi)
  • Hexylene Glycol
03 ⚗️
Ethanolamines

Multifunctional amines serving as key building blocks in gas treatment, detergent formulation, agricultural chemicals, and personal care products.

  • Monoethanolamine (MEA)
  • Diethanolamine (DEA)
  • Triethanolamine (TEA)
04 🔬
Glycol Ethers

High-performance solvents critical to coatings, inks, cleaners, and semiconductor manufacturing. Offered across the full ethylene and propylene glycol ether series.

  • Ethylene Glycol Monobutyl Ether (EB)
  • Diethylene Glycol Monobutyl Ether (DB)
  • Propylene Glycol Monomethyl Ether (PM)
  • Dipropylene Glycol Monomethyl Ether (DPM)
  • PMA, TPM, TB
05 🧬
Ethyleneamines

Versatile polyamine intermediates used in epoxy curing agents, chelating agents, fuel additives, and textile auxiliaries across a broad industrial spectrum.

  • Aminoethylethanolamine (AEEA)
  • Ethylenediamine (EDA)
  • Diethylenetriamine (DETA)
  • Tetraethylenepentamine (TEPA)
  • Triethylenetrtramine (TETA)
06 🏭
Plasticizers

Non-phthalate and specialty plasticizers that enhance flexibility, durability, and processing performance in PVC compounds, wire and cable insulation, and film applications.

  • DOTP
  • DINP
  • TOTM
  • DOA
  • DINCH / DHIN · TPEG · VPEG
07 🌿
C5 Products

Reactive diene and olefin monomers derived from steam cracker C5 streams, serving the synthetic rubber, resin, and adhesive industries with precision-spec material.

  • Isoprene (IPM)
  • Dicyclopentadiene (DCPD)
  • Piperylene
  • Isoamylene
08 🧴
Surfactants & Acids

Surface-active agents and organic acid intermediates for detergent manufacturing, agrochemical formulation, and industrial cleaning — supplied to technical and industrial-grade specifications.

  • Alkyl / Fatty Alcohol Ethoxylates
  • Castor Oil Ethoxylates
  • SMCA / MCA
  • LABSA / DDBSA
  • Isophthalic Acid
09 ⚡
Specialties

A curated selection of high-value specialty chemicals for oilfield treatment, pharmaceutical synthesis, and advanced industrial processes — including custom-formulated triazine blends.

  • Alkyl Pyridine Quat
  • Alkyl Pyridine
  • Isopropyl Alcohol (IPA)
  • Pentaerythritol
  • MEA Triazine 50% & 78%
